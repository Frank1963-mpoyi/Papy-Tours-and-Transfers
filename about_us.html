About Cape Town Adventures

Your local experts in Cape Town tourism

Our Story

Founded by passionate locals in 2015, Cape Town Adventures was born from a desire to share the beauty and diversity of our incredible city with the world. We believe in sustainable tourism that benefits both visitors and local communities.

With over 8 years of experience, our team of certified guides knows every corner of Cape Town - from the iconic Table Mountain to hidden gems only locals know about. We're committed to creating personalized experiences that go beyond the typical tourist path.

Why Choose Us

  • Local Expertise: Born and raised in Cape Town with deep local knowledge
  • Personalized Experiences: Tailored tours to match your interests
  • Sustainable Tourism: Supporting local communities and conservation
  • 5-Star Service: Dedicated to making your experience unforgettable
